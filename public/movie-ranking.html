The Best Movies According to Brian

My top 3 movies of all-time

Scott Pilgram vs. the World

This might be my favourite movie of all time! It's a movie based off a comic book that takes place in a weird video game world. I like that it works in the style of the old 8-bit fighting games that I played as a kid.

John Wick

I think that every bro in the country loves this movie. The action and the fight scenes are very well done. Old action movies were very stale for a long time and I think that John Wick really imporved on the genre.

Event Horizon

I love horror movies. I love sci-fi. This movie brings both of genres together in a disturbing way.
